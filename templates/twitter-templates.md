# Twitter/X Outreach Templates

---

## Cold DM

Hey [Name]! I run The Build Up — we tell real founder stories. No PR fluff.

[Specific thing about their business] caught my eye. Would love to feature you. 20 min casual chat. Interested?

---

## Reply-then-DM Strategy (recommended)

**Step 1:** Reply to their tweet with genuine value or insight — NOT a pitch. Do this 2-3 times over a week.

**Step 2:** After they engage with your replies, send this DM:

Hey! Loved your take on [topic]. I run The Build Up — we go deep on how founders actually built their businesses. Your story with [Company] would be perfect. Quick chat?

## Twitter/X Best Practices

- The reply-then-DM strategy has 2-3x higher response rates than cold DMs
- Create a Twitter List of target founders so you see their tweets daily
- Engage authentically — founders can smell a pitch from a mile away
- Best times for DMs: weekday mornings, avoid weekends
- Keep DMs under 280 characters if possible
