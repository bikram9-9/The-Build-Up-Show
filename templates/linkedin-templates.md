# LinkedIn Outreach Templates

---

## Connection Request Message

Hey [Name] — I run The Build Up where we feature founders making real money with real businesses. No unicorn hype. Would love to connect and hear your [Company] story.

---

## After Connection Accepted

Thanks for connecting! I'd love to feature your story on The Build Up — especially [specific detail about their business/revenue/journey].

It's a casual 20 min convo about how you actually built it. No prep needed. Down?

---

## LinkedIn Best Practices

- Send 10-15 connection requests per day max
- Engage with their posts for a week before sending a DM (like, comment with value)
- Do NOT use automation tools — LinkedIn will ban your account
- Best times: Tuesday-Thursday, 8am-10am their timezone
- Keep connection messages under 300 characters
